About Us

CIS Bakery was founded in the fall semester of 2018 by Taia, Trey, and Katelyn. We offer a variety of bakery items such as cakes, cupcakes and cookies. We are located in several different places in Iowa. Check out our store locations page for more info about our locations. We are open Monday through Saturday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. If you have any questions, please call 321-456-7890 or email us @ cisbakery@email.com.
