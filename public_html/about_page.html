About Us

CIS Bakery was founded in the fall semester of 2018 by Taia, Trey, and Katelyn. We offer many bakery items such as cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. We are located in several different places in Iowa. Check out our store locations page for more info about our locations.

We are open Monday through Saturday from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. If you have any questions, please call 123-456-7890 or reach out by email at cisbakery@email.com.
